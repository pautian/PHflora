Common Name: PH Acacia

Scientific Name: Acacia Confusa
Order: Fabales
Family: Fabaceae

Small Philippine Acacia is native to the Philippines. The fast-growing tree can reach heights up to 15 m with a thick leafy canopy. The thick leaves block sunlight from reaching native plants. The tree also has a deep taproot that spreads out in search of water in dry conditions. It can easily deplete moisture from the soil, depriving other species. It spreads by seeding and often grows in clusters further pushing native plant life out of its habitat.
