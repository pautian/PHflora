Common Name: Philippine lily

Scientific Name: Lilium Philippinense
Order: Liliales
Family: Liliaceae

The native range of this species is Taiwan to Phlippines. The plant's flowers are white trumpet-shaped, known to emit a fragrant odor. It is a bulbous geophyte and grows primarily in the subtropical biome. The Philippines lily cannot grow in crowded conditions and requires lots of water to survive in the warm conditions. After removal from soil, lilies can only last about 14 days.
