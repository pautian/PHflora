Common Name: Sampaguita

Scientific Name: Jasminum Sambac
Order: Lamiales
Family: Oleaceae

Sampaguita, the national flower of Philippines, is grown in the tropics, mostly for its rich fragrance. It is a flowering shrub with small fragrant white flowers and is a species of jasmine. Unlike other species of jasmine, sampaguita is a tropical evergreen shrub that flowers throughout the year in tropical climate. Although the size of its flowers is smaller than some other species, it is much more fragrant than the others.
